<p><ul><li>Membrane Processes</li><li>Thermal Treatment</li><li>Ion Exchange</li><li>Capacitive Deionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas water purification system market encompasses several key technologies for treating wastewater. Membrane processes, like reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration, separate contaminants using semi-permeable membranes. Thermal treatment employs heat to evaporate water, leaving impurities behind. Ion exchange systems utilize resin to remove dissolved ions, effectively softening water or removing specific pollutants. Capacitive deionization captures charged particles between two electrodes when electrical potential is applied, providing an efficient method for water decontamination. These technologies cater to various purification needs in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
