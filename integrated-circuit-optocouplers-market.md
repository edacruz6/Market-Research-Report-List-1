<p><p>The integrated circuit optocoupler market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Phototransistor** - These utilize a phototransistor to transmit signals, offering moderate speed and good isolation.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Photoelectric Darlington** - Combining phototransistor characteristics with a Darlington pair, they provide high sensitivity and gain.</p><p>3. **Photoelectric Thyristor** - Designed for high-power applications, these optocouplers enable control of AC loads.</p><p>4. **Photoelectric Triac** - Similar to thyristors, they're used for AC switching, providing bidirectional control in applications like lighting and motor control.</p></p>
