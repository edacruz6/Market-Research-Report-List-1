<p><p>The fiberglass silicone cloth market is divided into two primary types based on coating: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided coating features a silicone layer applied to only one side of the fiberglass, offering moderate protection against heat, chemicals, and abrasion, making it suitable for various industrial uses. In contrast, double-sided coating involves silicone treatment on both sides, enhancing durability and insulation, thus providing superior resistance and versatility in extreme conditions, ideal for high-performance applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
