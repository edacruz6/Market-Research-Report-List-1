<p><p>The sheath catheter market encompasses various types, including femoral artery sheath and radial artery sheath, each serving distinct clinical needs. The femoral artery sheath is primarily used for procedures involving the femoral artery, providing access for interventions such as angioplasty or catheterization. In contrast, the radial artery sheath is employed for accessing the radial artery, often favored for its lower complication rates. The "other" category includes specialized sheaths designed for unique vascular access requirements, catering to diverse medical applications.</p></p>
