<p><ul><li>CCD Chip</li><li>CMOS Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital photosensitive material market primarily consists of two key types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) chips and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) chips. CCD chips are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them ideal for applications like professional photography and scientific imaging. In contrast, CMOS chips offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integrated features, making them popular in consumer electronics such as smartphones and webcams. Both technologies serve distinct needs in imaging applications, driving the market's growth.</p></p>
