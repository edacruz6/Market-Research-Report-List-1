<p><p>The automotive parts zinc die casting market comprises several techniques: </p><p>1. **Pressure Die Casting** involves injecting molten zinc into a mold under high pressure, producing precise parts quickly.</p><p>2. **Vacuum Die Casting** reduces air entrapment, enhancing surface quality and minimizing defects.</p><p>3. **Squeeze Die Casting** applies pressure to the zinc, ensuring better material flow and density in complex shapes.</p><p>4. **Semi-Solid Die Casting** utilizes partially solidified metal, offering improved mechanical properties and reduced porosity.</p><p>5. **Others** may include specialized or hybrid methods tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
