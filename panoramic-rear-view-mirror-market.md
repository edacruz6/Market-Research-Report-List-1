<p><p>The Panoramic Rear View Mirror market includes two main types: Plane Mirror and Convex Mirror. Plane Mirrors provide a flat, undistorted reflection, offering a true-to-life view of the rear, ideal for precise distance judgment. Convex Mirrors, with their curved surface, provide a wider field of view, reducing blind spots but slightly distorting the image. Both types cater to safety needs, with Plane Mirrors focusing on accuracy and Convex Mirrors emphasizing broader visibility, enhancing driver awareness in different driving scenarios.</p></p>
