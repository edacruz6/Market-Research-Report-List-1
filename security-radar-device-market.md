<p><p>The Security Radar Device Market is primarily segmented into two types: imaging radar and non-imaging radar. Imaging radar provides detailed visual representation of targets, allowing for enhanced object recognition and situational awareness, making it ideal for surveillance applications. In contrast, non-imaging radar focuses on detecting and tracking objects without forming detailed images, typically used for motion detection and perimeter security. Both types cater to various security needs across sectors such as defense, transportation, and critical infrastructure protection.</p></p>
