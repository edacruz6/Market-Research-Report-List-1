<p><p>Hyper-Converged Infrastructure (HCI) solutions are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based HCI leverages virtualization and storage in a hosted environment, providing scalability and flexibility, ideal for businesses seeking to minimize physical infrastructure management. In contrast, on-premises HCI integrates compute, storage, and networking within a local data center, offering organizations full control over their infrastructure and compliance needs. Both models enable efficient resource utilization and simplified management but cater to different operational preferences.</p></p>
