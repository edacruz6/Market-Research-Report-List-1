<p><p>Eccentric disk valves are crucial components in fluid control systems, characterized by their unique design that enhances flow and reduces turbulence. The market can be categorized into wafer type and lug type valves. Wafer-type valves are compact, easily installed between flanges, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, lug-type valves feature threaded lugs for bolting to the piping, providing enhanced stability and ease of maintenance. Both types cater to specific operational needs in industries like water treatment, chemicals, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
