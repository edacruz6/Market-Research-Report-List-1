<p><p>The night vision goggle market primarily consists of two types: LLL (Lightweight, Low-Light) night vision goggles and infrared night vision goggles. LLL goggles amplify ambient light to enhance visibility in low-light conditions, making them suitable for tactical operations and outdoor activities. Meanwhile, infrared night vision goggles utilize thermal imaging technology to detect heat signatures, allowing visibility in complete darkness. Both types cater to military, law enforcement, and civilian sectors, with applications ranging from surveillance to wildlife observation.</p></p>
