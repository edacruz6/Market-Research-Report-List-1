<p><strong>The Pelvic Plating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Locking</li><li>Non-Locking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pelvic Plating System market is divided into two main types: locking and non-locking plates. Locking plates feature screws that lock into the plate, providing enhanced stability and improved fixation in complex fractures, ideal for cases with compromised bone quality. Non-locking plates, on the other hand, allow screws to compress the plate against the bone, offering more flexibility but less stability. Both types are essential in orthopedic surgery for effective treatment of pelvic injuries, each serving specific clinical needs.</p></p>
