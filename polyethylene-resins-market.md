<p><p>Polyethylene resins are categorized mainly into four types based on density and molecular structure. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and used in packaging films. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers strength and rigidity, suitable for containers and piping. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) provides enhanced toughness and is used in stretch films and bags. Other types include Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) andSpecific Copolymer formulations, each tailored for diverse applications, from automotive to construction, ensuring versatility in the market.</p></p>
