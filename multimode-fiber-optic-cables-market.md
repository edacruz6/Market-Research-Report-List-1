<p><strong>The Multimode Fiber Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Index</li><li>Gradient Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multimode fiber optic cables market is primarily categorized into two types: step index and graded index fibers. Step index cables feature a uniform core diameter with a distinct boundary between the core and cladding, offering simplified light transmission but higher modal dispersion. In contrast, graded index cables have a varying refractive index within the core, reducing modal dispersion and enhancing bandwidth over longer distances. Both types serve diverse applications in telecommunications, data centers, and networks, catering to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
