<p><strong>The Oil-trunk Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Pump</li><li>Rod Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-trunk pumps market comprises various types, primarily tube pumps and rod pumps. Tube pumps utilize a flexible tube to transport fluids through mechanical action, offering precise flow control and minimal friction. In contrast, rod pumps employ a rod mechanism to lift oil from sub-surface reservoirs, effectively managing high-viscosity fluids. Both types cater to different operational needs in oil extraction and transportation, with tube pumps favored for their efficiency in low-pressure scenarios and rod pumps for their robustness in challenging conditions.</p></p>
