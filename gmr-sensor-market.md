<p><p>The GMR sensor market comprises several types, each catering to specific applications. Standard Multilayer (ML) sensors are widely used for general magnetic field sensing. High Temperature Multilayer (HTM) sensors operate effectively in elevated temperatures, while Low Hysteresis High Temperature Multilayer (LHHTM) sensors minimize hysteresis effects, enhancing accuracy. Spin Valve (SV) sensors offer high sensitivity and are ideal for data storage. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and specialized sensors that address niche applications, further diversifying the market.</p></p>
