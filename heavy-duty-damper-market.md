<p><p>The heavy-duty damper market is primarily classified into two types: hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic dampers use fluid mechanics to absorb energy and control movement, making them suitable for high-load applications. They offer precise control and are often used in industrial settings. Pneumatic dampers, on the other hand, utilize compressed air to regulate flow and pressure, providing a lightweight solution ideal for environments with fluctuating conditions. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining system efficiency and performance across various industries.</p></p>
