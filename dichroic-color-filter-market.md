<p><strong>The Dichroic Color Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subtractive Dichroic Filters</li><li>Additive Dichroic Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dichroic color filter market consists of two primary types: subtractive and additive dichroic filters. Subtractive dichroic filters work by absorbing specific wavelengths of light while allowing others to pass through, resulting in color effects suitable for applications like photography and stage lighting. In contrast, additive dichroic filters mix various colors of transmitted light, enhancing visual displays and creating vibrant hues in applications such as digital signage and architectural lighting. Both filters are essential for precise color control in various industries.</p></p>
