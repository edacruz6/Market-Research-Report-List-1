<p><strong>The Stained Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acid-based Staining</li><li>Water-based Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stained concrete market primarily includes two types: acid-based staining and water-based staining. Acid-based staining reacts with the minerals in the concrete to create a translucent, variegated finish, often resulting in earthy tones. This method is durable and often used for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based staining offers a wider range of colors and is easier to apply, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor projects. It dries quickly and is more environmentally friendly, appealing to modern design trends.</p></p>
