<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing Systems encompass various techniques to create parts layer by layer using metal powders. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) employs high-powered lasers to melt and fuse metal particles, enabling intricate designs with high density. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) uses an electron beam in a vacuum to achieve similar results, offering faster build times and the ability to work with refractory metals. Other methods include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, each varying in materials, processes, and applications.</p></p>
