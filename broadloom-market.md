<p><p>The broadloom market features three main types of carpets: cut pile, loop pile, and cut-and-loop pile. Cut pile carpets provide a soft, plush surface by cutting the yarn loops, creating a luxurious feel. Loop pile carpets, on the other hand, maintain uncut loops for durability and texture, ideal for high-traffic areas. Cut-and-loop pile combines both techniques, offering varied textures and patterns, enhancing design versatility while balancing comfort and durability for different settings and preferences.</p></p>
