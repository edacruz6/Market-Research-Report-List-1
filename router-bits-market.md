<p><p>Router bits are categorized primarily by shank size, with the most common types being 1/2" shank bits and 1/4" shank bits. 1/2" shank bits are generally preferred for professional use due to their stability and ability to handle higher speeds and heavier cuts. In contrast, 1/4" shank bits are often favored by hobbyists for lighter tasks and portability. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized bits like indexed, dovetail, and spiral bits, catering to specific applications and user preferences.</p></p>
