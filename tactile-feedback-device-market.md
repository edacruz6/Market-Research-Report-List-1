<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tactile feedback device market includes several actuator types. Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators create vibrations using an off-centered weight on a motor, producing a strong, constant feedback effect. Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) utilize a voice coil mechanism, offering precise and rapid vibrations with lower power consumption, ideal for nuanced feedback. Other market segments may include piezoelectric actuators, pneumatic devices, and haptic feedback technology in wearable devices, enhancing user experience across various applications from mobile devices to gaming and automotive interfaces.</p></p>
