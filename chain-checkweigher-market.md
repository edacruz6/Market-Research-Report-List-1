<p><strong>The Chain Checkweigher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chain checkweigher market includes two primary types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously measure the weight of products as they move along a conveyor belt, ensuring efficient and real-time monitoring suitable for high-speed production lines. In contrast, intermittent checkweighers pause products at specific intervals to assess their weight, providing more accurate readings for delicate items. Both types play crucial roles in quality control and compliance across various industries, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing waste.</p></p>
