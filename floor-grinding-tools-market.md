<p><p>The floor grinding tools market features two primary types: dry polishing and wet polishing. Dry polishing employs diamond abrasives without water, providing convenience and minimizing mess, making it ideal for indoor environments. Conversely, wet polishing uses water to cool the surface and reduce dust, resulting in a smoother finish and enhanced durability. Each method serves different application needs; dry is preferred for quick, efficient projects while wet is favored for deeper refinishing tasks in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
