<p><ul><li>Spot Welding</li><li>Seam Welding</li><li>Projection Welding</li><li>Butt Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistance welding device market encompasses several key types. Spot welding connects metal pieces at specific points using heat generated by electrical resistance, ideal for sheet metal fabrication. Seam welding creates continuous welds along edges, suitable for containers and piping. Projection welding utilizes raised features to focus heat on specific areas, enhancing joint strength and precision. Butt welding joins two metal ends directly, commonly used in manufacturing and automotive applications. Each method serves distinct industrial needs, driving market diversity.</p></p>
