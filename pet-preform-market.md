<p><p>The PET preform market features two primary types: non-crystallized neck finish preforms and crystallized neck finish preforms. Non-crystallized neck finish preforms are designed for standard applications, ensuring a lightweight and cost-effective solution for bottling. In contrast, crystallized neck finish preforms undergo a heating process to enhance strength and thermal resistance, making them suitable for hot-fill applications and products requiring higher durability. These types cater to varying industry needs, from beverages to packaging solutions for food and household items.</p></p>
