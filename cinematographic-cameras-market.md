<p><p>The cinematographic cameras market comprises several key types: ENG cameras, cinema cameras, and EFP cameras. ENG (Electronic News Gathering) cameras are compact, designed for fast-paced news environments, allowing quick deployment and easy operation. Cinema cameras are high-end devices focused on film production, offering superior image quality and flexibility for creative storytelling. EFP (Electronic Field Production) cameras bridge the gap, used for multi-camera setups in events and broadcasts, combining portability with professional-grade features for live television productions.</p></p>
