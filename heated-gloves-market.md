<p><p>The heated gloves market is primarily segmented into two types: chemical heated gloves and electric heated gloves. Chemical heated gloves utilize exothermic reactions from special materials to generate warmth, making them ideal for short-term use in extreme conditions. In contrast, electric heated gloves incorporate battery-powered heating elements, offering adjustable warmth and longer-lasting heat. Both types cater to outdoor enthusiasts, industrial workers, and individuals in cold environments, enhancing comfort and performance during activities such as skiing, hiking, and construction work.</p></p>
