<p><p>The spray texture market is primarily categorized into water-based and water-resistant types. In contrast, water-resistant spray textures offer enhanced durability and moisture protection, ideal for high-humidity areas such as bathrooms and kitchens. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, with water-based options focusing on ease of use and water-resistant options emphasizing longevity and performance in challenging conditions.</p></p>
