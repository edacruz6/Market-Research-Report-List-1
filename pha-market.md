PHBHx (Polyhydroxybutyrate-co-hexanoate) offers enhanced mechanical properties, ideal for durable products. PHB4B (Polyhydroxybutyrate-co-4-hydroxybutyrate) provides elasticity and biodegradability, used in textiles and agriculture. Each variant caters to specific market needs, balancing biodegradability, mechanical properties, and cost-effectiveness for sustainable applications.</p></p>
