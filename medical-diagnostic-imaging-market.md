<p><p>The medical and diagnostic imaging market comprises various types, including X-ray, Computed Tomography (CT), ultrasound, MRI, and nuclear imaging equipment. X-ray machines provide quick imaging for bone and soft tissue assessment. CT combines X-ray technology to create detailed cross-sectional images. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time imaging, often in obstetrics. MRI employs magnetic fields for precise soft tissue visualization. Nuclear imaging utilizes radioactive materials to evaluate organ function and diagnose conditions, offering unique insights into metabolic processes.</p></p>
