<p><ul><li>Combo Amplifiers</li><li>Head Amplifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guitar amplifier market primarily consists of two main types: combo amplifiers and head amplifiers. Combo amplifiers combine the amplifier and speaker in a single unit, offering portability and convenience, making them ideal for practice and small gigs. Head amplifiers, on the other hand, consist of the amplifier unit only and require separate speaker cabinets, providing greater versatility in sound customization and power handling, often preferred by professional musicians for larger performances and studio recordings.</p></p>
