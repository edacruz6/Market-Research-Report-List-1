<p><p>The digital telemetry system market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel telemetry. Single-channel telemetry focuses on transmitting data from one sensor or source, making it ideal for straightforward applications where limited data needs to be monitored. Multi-channel telemetry, on the other hand, supports multiple sensors simultaneously, allowing for comprehensive data collection and analysis from various sources. This versatility makes multi-channel systems suitable for complex environments, such as healthcare or industrial monitoring, where diverse data points are critical for decision-making.</p></p>
