<p><strong>The Modified Bitumen Roofing Syestem Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBS Modified Bitumen Roofing System</li><li>APP Modified Bitumen Roofing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified bitumen roofing systems are categorized into two main types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS modified bitumen offers flexibility and excellent performance in cold temperatures, making it ideal for diverse climates. In contrast, APP modified bitumen is known for its heat resistance and durability, making it suitable for warm regions. Both types enhance the waterproofing and structural integrity of roofs, providing long-lasting protection against harsh weather conditions, thus catering to various architectural and climatic needs.</p></p>
