<p><p>The lighter market is primarily divided into two types: gas lighters and liquid lighters. Liquid lighters, on the other hand, typically use lighter fluid, providing a longer-lasting flame and refillable design. Both types cater to consumers' diverse needs, with gas lighters preferred for portability and liquid lighters favored for durability.</p></p>
