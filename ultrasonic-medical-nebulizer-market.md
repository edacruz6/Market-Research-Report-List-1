<p><p>The Ultrasonic Medical Nebulizer market is primarily categorized into handheld and trolley types. They are compact and ideal for patients needing chronic respiratory treatment. In contrast, trolley-type nebulizers are larger, often used in clinical settings. These devices are mounted on mobile carts, allowing healthcare professionals to efficiently treat multiple patients, delivering precise medication dosages in hospitals or clinics.</p></p>
