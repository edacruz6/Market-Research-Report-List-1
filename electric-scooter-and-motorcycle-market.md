<p><p>The electric scooter and motorcycle market features two primary battery types: Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) and Lithium-Ion (Li-Ion). SLA batteries are cost-effective and robust, suitable for short-range and lower-speed applications but are heavier and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, Li-Ion batteries offer higher energy density, lighter weight, and longer lifecycle, making them ideal for longer commutes and faster vehicles. The choice between these battery types significantly impacts performance, range, and overall user experience in electric mobility.</p></p>
