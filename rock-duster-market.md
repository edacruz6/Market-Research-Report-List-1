<p><p>The Rock Duster market consists of two primary types: hydraulic and electric. On the other hand, electric rock dusters rely on electric power to operate, offering a quieter and more energy-efficient option, ideal for well-ventilated mines. Both types serve the essential function of improving air quality and enhancing safety in mining operations by minimizing dust exposure.</p></p>
