<p><ul><li>Low Energy Medical Cyclotron</li><li>High Energy Medical Cyclotron Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical cyclotron market is segmented into low energy and high energy types based on their particle acceleration capabilities. Low energy medical cyclotrons primarily produce isotopes for diagnostic imaging, generating protons at energies around 10-20 MeV, suitable for applications like PET scans. In contrast, high energy medical cyclotrons operate at energies above 20 MeV, enabling the production of a wider range of therapeutic isotopes and supporting both diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in nuclear medicine, enhancing treatment options for patients.</p></p>
