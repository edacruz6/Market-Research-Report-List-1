<p><strong>The Spray Foam Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray foam equipment market consists mainly of two types: closed cell and open cell foam. Closed cell foam has a denser structure, offering high insulation value and moisture resistance, making it ideal for exterior applications and areas requiring structural integrity. Open cell foam, on the other hand, has a softer, more flexible composition, providing sound absorption and thermal insulation, making it suitable for interior applications. Both types cater to diverse insulation needs across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
