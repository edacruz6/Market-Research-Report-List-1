<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging (WLP) Inspection Systems are critical for ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices. Optical-based systems use visible light to detect surface defects, alignment issues, and structural anomalies, offering high precision for visible layer inspections. Infrared (IR) type systems utilize infrared light to inspect deeper layers and internal structures, identifying hidden defects like voids or delamination. Both systems are essential for advanced packaging technologies, with optical focusing on surface quality and IR addressing internal integrity.</p></p>
