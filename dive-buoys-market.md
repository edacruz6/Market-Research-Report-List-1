<p><strong>The Dive Buoys Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMB</li><li>DSMB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dive buoys are critical tools in underwater diving operations, categorized mainly into two market types: SMB (Surface Marker Buoy) and DSMB (Delayed Surface Marker Buoy). SMBs are used by divers to signal their position from the water's surface, enhancing safety and visibility. In contrast, DSMBs are deployed from underwater to provide a surface marker when divers ascend, allowing boats to locate them accurately. Both types are essential for navigation and safety in various diving conditions, catering to recreational and professional divers alike.</p></p>
