<p><p>The Agricultural Dyes and Pigments Market encompasses two primary types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants used for coloring various agricultural products, such as seeds and fabrics, ensuring visual appeal and identification. Conversely, pigments are insoluble colorants providing vivid, long-lasting coloration to agricultural materials, including coatings and plastics. Both types play essential roles in enhancing product aesthetics, improving chemical resistance, and facilitating branding in the agricultural sector, contributing to market growth and innovation.</p></p>
