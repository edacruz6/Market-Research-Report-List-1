<p><p>The intracorporeal lithotripsy market encompasses various techniques for fragmenting stones within the urinary tract. Mechanical lithotripsy employs a device to physically break stones. Electrohydraulic lithotripsy uses shock waves generated by a spark gap, while laser lithotripsy employs focused laser energy for precision fragmentation. Ultrasonic lithotripsy utilizes high-frequency sound waves to disintegrate stones. Additionally, the "Others" category includes innovative or less common techniques. Each method varies in effectiveness, safety, and application, catering to specific patient needs.</p></p>
