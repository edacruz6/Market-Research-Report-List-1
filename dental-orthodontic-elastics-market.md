<p><p>The dental orthodontic elastics market comprises various types designed for specific correction needs. "Class II Elastics" are used to correct overbites by pulling the lower jaw forward. "Class III Elastics" address underbites by stabilizing the upper jaw. "Vertical Elastics" focus on adjusting vertical relationships, maintaining proper bite alignment. "Front Cross Elastics" assist in correcting misalignment between the front teeth. "Others" may include specialty elastics for unique orthodontic requirements, catering to diverse patient needs and treatment plans.</p></p>
