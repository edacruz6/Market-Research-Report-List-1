<p><p>The ligament prostheses market includes allograft and synthetic prostheses. Allograft prostheses utilize donated human tissue, providing a natural option for ligament reconstruction with good integration into the body. In contrast, synthetic prostheses are man-made materials designed to replicate ligament function, offering durability and reduced risk of disease transmission. Each type serves distinct needs in orthopedic and sports medicine, driven by factors such as patient preference, injury severity, and the desired recovery outcome.</p></p>
