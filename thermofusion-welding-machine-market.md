<p><p>The thermofusion welding machine market comprises four main types: Mechanical, Hydraulic, CNC-guided, and Manual. </p><p>Mechanical machines use a system of gears and levers to create the necessary pressure and heat for welding. Hydraulic machines utilize fluid power to generate force, offering greater control in larger applications. CNC-guided machines are automated, providing precision and consistency through computer control. Manual machines require operator involvement for the welding process, making them suitable for smaller or less complex tasks. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
