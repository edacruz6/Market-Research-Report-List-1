<p><p>The analog insulation testers market comprises two primary types based on voltage: AC voltage and DC voltage testers. AC voltage testers measure insulation resistance using alternating current, which is effective for evaluating the insulation of equipment operating in AC systems. In contrast, DC voltage testers utilize direct current to assess insulation resistance, providing more accurate readings in certain applications, such as testing high-voltage insulation. Both types are essential for ensuring electrical safety and equipment reliability across various industries.</p></p>
