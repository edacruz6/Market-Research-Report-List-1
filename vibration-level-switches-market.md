<p><p>Vibration level switches are essential in various industries for detecting material levels. The two main types are vibrating fork and vibrating rod switches. Vibrating fork switches consist of two tines that vibrate at a specific frequency; when immersed in material, the vibration frequency changes, indicating level detection. In contrast, vibrating rod switches utilize a single rod, vibrating in a similar manner to detect level changes. Both types offer reliable, maintenance-free operation, making them suitable for various applications, including liquids and solids.</p></p>
