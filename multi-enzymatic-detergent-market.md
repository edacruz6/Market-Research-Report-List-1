<p><p>The multi-enzymatic detergent market is divided into two primary types: neutral pH detergents and alkaline detergents. Neutral pH detergents are formulated to work effectively at a pH close to 7, making them suitable for cleaning delicate fabrics and surfaces without causing damage. Alkaline detergents, on the other hand, have a higher pH and are designed to tackle tough stains and grease, making them ideal for industrial and heavy-duty cleaning applications. Both types leverage enzymes for enhanced cleaning efficiency.</p></p>
