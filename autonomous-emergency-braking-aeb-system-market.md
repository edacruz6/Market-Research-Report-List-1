<p><p>The Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) system market is categorized by sensor types: Camera, Fusion, LiDAR, and Radar. Camera-based AEB uses visual data to detect obstacles, offering cost-effective solutions. Fusion systems combine multiple sensors (e.g., cameras and radar) for enhanced accuracy and reliability. LiDAR-based AEB employs laser technology for precise object detection, ideal for high-performance applications. Radar-based systems use radio waves to detect objects, excelling in adverse weather conditions. Each type caters to varying needs, balancing cost, accuracy, and environmental adaptability in automotive safety.</p></p>
