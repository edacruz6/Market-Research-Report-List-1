<p><strong>The Flat Panel Display Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation</li><li>Photolithography (Exposure)</li><li>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD)</li><li>Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA)</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)</li><li>Wet Etch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat panel display equipment market comprises various technologies used in the manufacturing of displays. Evaporation involves depositing material by vaporizing it. Photolithography (Exposure) utilizes light to transfer patterns on substrates, while Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) allows deposition through vaporized materials. Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA) uses lasers to enhance the properties of thin films. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) creates solid materials from gaseous precursors, and Wet Etch involves using liquid chemicals to remove layers from substrates, shaping circuit patterns.</p></p>
