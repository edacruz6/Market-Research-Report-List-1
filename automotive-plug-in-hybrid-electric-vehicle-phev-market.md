<p><strong>The Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Plug-in</li><li>Parallel or Hybrid Plug-in</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) markets can be categorized into three main types: series, parallel, and hybrid plug-in configurations. In a series plug-in, the electric motor drives the vehicle while the internal combustion engine acts solely as a generator, providing energy to the battery. The parallel plug-in integrates both the electric motor and the engine, allowing them to work together for propulsion. Hybrid plug-in combines features of both, enabling flexible operation modes for efficiency and performance.</p></p>
