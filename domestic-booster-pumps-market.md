<p><p>The domestic booster pumps market primarily consists of two types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for low to moderate pressure applications, effectively moving water through a single impeller, making them ideal for household needs. Multi-stage pumps, on the other hand, utilize multiple impellers to generate higher pressure, making them suitable for larger homes and applications requiring elevated water delivery. Both types enhance water pressure, ensuring efficient water supply in residential settings.</p></p>
