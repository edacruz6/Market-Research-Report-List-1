<p><p>The IoT RFID electronic tags market is categorized into passive and active RFID types. Passive RFID tags are energy-efficient, relying on the reader's signal for power, and are typically used for inventory tracking and supply chain management. In contrast, active RFID tags contain a battery, enabling them to transmit signals over greater distances and providing real-time tracking capabilities, making them ideal for asset management and high-value item monitoring. Both types enhance operational efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
