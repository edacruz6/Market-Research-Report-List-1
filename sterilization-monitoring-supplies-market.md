<p><p>The sterilization monitoring supplies market comprises three main types: biological, chemical, and mechanical monitoring. Biological monitoring involves using living organisms or spores to verify the effectiveness of sterilization processes. Chemical monitoring utilizes chemical indicators to change color or form in response to specific sterilization conditions, offering immediate feedback. Mechanical monitoring includes the assessment of physical parameters such as temperature and pressure during sterilization cycles, ensuring adherence to operational standards. Together, these methods ensure thorough sterilization and patient safety.</p></p>
