<p><p>The automotive airless tires market includes bias and radial tires, both designed for durability and performance without the need for air pressure. Radial tires have perpendicular ply layers, providing better fuel efficiency, smoother rides, and improved traction, commonly used in passenger cars. Both types eliminate the risk of punctures, enhancing safety and reducing maintenance, making them suitable for diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
