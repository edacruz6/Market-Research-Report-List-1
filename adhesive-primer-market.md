<p><p>The adhesive primer market encompasses several types, each influencing bonding performance in various applications. Silane primors enhance adhesion on substrates like glass and metals by forming durable bonds. Maleic anhydride primers improve adhesion in thermoplastic and thermosetting polymers. Polyolefin primers facilitate bonding with polyolefin surfaces, crucial for automotive and packaging industries. Titanate primers promote adhesion on challenging surfaces, while zirconate primers are used for specialty applications requiring high-temperature resistance. Each type caters to specific material compatibility and performance needs.</p></p>
